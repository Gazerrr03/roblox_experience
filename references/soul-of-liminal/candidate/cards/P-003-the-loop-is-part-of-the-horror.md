## Principle
Repeated rounds and revival should be part of the fiction, not an unexamined gamey reset.

## Explanation
If players are controlled, replaceable exploration bodies, then death, revival, rewards, and round restarts all gain narrative weight. The loop itself becomes part of the horror.

## Counterexamples / Anti-goals
- treating revival as a pure meta convenience with no fiction
- explaining everything too early with explicit exposition
- making clone replacement feel emotionally neutral
