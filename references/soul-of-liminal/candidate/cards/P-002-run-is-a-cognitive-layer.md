## Principle
`Run` should function as a narrative and information layer that changes how players interpret `Maze`.

## Explanation
The player should enter `Maze` with a better mental model, not just a route marker. `Run` earns its existence by shaping expectations, suspicion, and decision quality.

## Counterexamples / Anti-goals
- making `Run` a pure corridor to the maze gate
- placing lore in `Run` that never matters inside `Maze`
- using `Run` only as a shop lobby
