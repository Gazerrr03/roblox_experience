## Current Candidate Thesis

`Liminal` is not mainly about becoming stronger.
It is about becoming less blind inside a system that treats human beings as expendable exploration bodies.

## Candidate Core Tensions

- survival vs obedience
- knowledge vs misinformation
- team cooperation vs hidden betrayal
- mission efficiency vs human cost
- visible rules vs half-understood rules

## Candidate Identity

The project currently feels closest to:

- horror expedition
- multi-round extraction loop
- information-driven survival
- narrative that turns gamey repetition into fiction
- camp-economy surface masking a darker memory-harvest loop

## Candidate Anti-Goals

The project should avoid collapsing into:

- a simple corridor prelude before the real game starts
- a generic loot run with horror wallpaper
- a pure stat-growth loop where knowledge barely matters
- a lore dump that explains too much too early
