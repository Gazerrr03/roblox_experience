## Principle
The main feeling of progression in `Liminal` should come from improved judgment, not just improved numbers.

## Explanation
Players are fragile, replaceable, and under pressure. The game becomes distinctive when players survive because they understand more, not simply because their build scales harder.

## Counterexamples / Anti-goals
- turning `Run` into a loot hallway
- solving every problem with stat buffs
- making clues feel optional because raw gear outclasses knowledge
